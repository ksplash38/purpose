Our services

Land Transport

Fastroute Delivery Services offers an efficient integrated transport system with multiple types of trucks available to support your trucking and distribution needs.

Ship Transport

Fastroute Delivery Services offer professional and high quality sea freight services, with personal escort which will enable you to transfer your cargo to its destination in the most secure and low priced manner.

Air Transport

Fastroute Delivery Services is equipped with the right expertise, experience and capacity to ensure the safe handling and appropriate transportation of your valuable shipments that may range from large scale manufacturing and sensitive equipment, engineering products, delicate materials to life animals.

Trucking Services

Charter Link can handle a variety of domestic & international trucking services to suit your needs. We offer our customers FTL, LTL & Flat bed.

Storage Service

We can assist customers with short and long term storage nationally in all our office worldwide, Whether you are looking for storage space as a short term stop gap or a long term.

Customs Brokerage

As a company, we are strengthening our relationship with Customs & Border Protection as well as other government agencies involved in the import process for the benefit of our customers.
